Welcome to EduSteps

A learning platform that supports students from primary school to university, guiding them step-by-step to find the most suitable and trusted teachers for their academic needs.

Our Services

We provide a clear place where students and parents can explore different teachers, see the subjects they teach, their grade levels, and basic information about each one. This makes it easier to choose a teacher for school support, private lessons, or extra practice at home.

How We Help You

Instead of searching in many different places, our website collects the important details in one page. Users can quickly compare teachers and find someone who matches the student’s needs and learning style, saving both time and effort for families.

How to Use the Website

First, the user browses or searches for teachers by subject or level. Then they can click “Book your session” on a teacher to open the booking page. On the booking page, the user chooses a subject from the dropdown and sees the available times in the schedule table. Users can explore teachers without logging in, but they cannot confirm a booking unless they are logged in.
